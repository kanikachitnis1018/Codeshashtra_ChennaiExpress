Welcome to Our Website

Our innovative solution leverages cutting-edge technology to provide accurate real-time detection of human posture and actions, with a specific focus on utilizing a pen with click functionality. While the context of using a pen is provided as a familiar example, our solution's primary objective is to address healthcare scenarios, emphasizing the precise detection and analysis required for medical devices such as asthma pumps. The solution offers efficiency and reliability, enabling the identification of object presence, usage, and related actions in real-time.

Pen Activation Analytics

Our system tracks and records the number of times the pen is activated (on) and deactivated (off) within a predefined timeframe. This feature provides valuable insights into pen usage patterns, facilitating data-driven decisions and optimizations.

Shake Detection and Analysis

With our integrated shake detection feature, the system can identify pre-action behaviors by detecting shaking movements of the pen before its use. It offers detailed analysis, including the count, frequency, and orientation of shakes per second, enhancing the accuracy of action detection and providing valuable insights into user behavior.

Guided Tour for User Understanding

To ensure effective utilization of the technology, our solution incorporates a guided tour feature. Users are provided with a step-by-step walkthrough of the system's functionalities, enabling them to understand and utilize the technology effectively. This feature enhances user experience and promotes user engagement by facilitating seamless interaction with the system.
