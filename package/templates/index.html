What is ASTHMA?

Asthma is a chronic inflammatory condition of the airways characterized by recurrent episodes of wheezing, breathlessness, chest tightness, and coughing. These symptoms typically occur at night or early in the morning, making it difficult for individuals to breathe properly. Asthma affects people of all ages, and while it cannot be cured, it can be managed effectively with proper treatment and lifestyle changes. Raising awareness about asthma is crucial to ensure early detection, proper management, and improved quality of life for individuals living with this condition.

How to Treat ASTHMA

Treatment for asthma typically involves long-term management strategies to control symptoms and prevent exacerbations. Common treatments include the use of inhalers (such as bronchodilators and corticosteroids), oral medications, and lifestyle modifications (such as avoiding triggers and maintaining a healthy lifestyle). It's essential for individuals with asthma to work closely with their healthcare providers to develop a personalized treatment plan that meets their specific needs and ensures optimal asthma control.

Precautions to Take Against ASTHMA

Taking precautions can help individuals with asthma manage their condition effectively and reduce the risk of exacerbations. Some essential precautions include avoiding exposure to known triggers (such as allergens, smoke, and pollutants), maintaining good indoor air quality, adhering to medication regimens as prescribed by healthcare providers, and having an asthma action plan in place for emergencies. Additionally, regular exercise and maintaining a healthy diet can contribute to overall asthma management and improve lung function.

Pen Activation Analytics

Our system tracks and records the number of times the pen is activated (on) and deactivated (off) within a predefined timeframe. This feature provides valuable insights into pen usage patterns, facilitating data-driven decisions and optimizations.

Shake Detection and Analysis

With our integrated shake detection feature, the system can identify pre-action behaviors by detecting shaking movements of the pen before its use. It offers detailed analysis, including the count, frequency, and orientation of shakes per second, enhancing the accuracy of action detection and providing valuable insights into user behavior.
